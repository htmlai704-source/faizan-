The primary leader of the Autobots is Optimus Prime, though other leaders like Rodimus Prime and Sentinel Prime have held the title. The title "Prime" is given to the leader and is associated with carrying the Matrix of Leadership, a powerful artifact that holds the wisdom of past Autobot leaders. Primary leader Optimus Prime: The most well-known and respected leader of the Autobots. He was formerly named Orion Pax and was chosen by Alpha Trion to lead the Autobots. He is the primary bearer of the Matrix of Leadership and is known for his nobility, humility, and good will. Other leaders Rodimus Prime: Took over leadership after Optimus Prime's death in The Transformers: The Movie. Sentinel Prime: Another significant leader in Autobot history. Nova Prime: An earlier leader who came before Optimus Prime.
